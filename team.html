Dr. Manirul Islam Khan

Managing Director

Dr. Manirul Islam Khan is the Managing Director of MTI Ltd. He directly oversees the whole MTI’s activities in Bangladesh and Thailand office. Mr. Khan has strong relation with different foreign international training institutions and universities. More than 16 years he has been involved with conducting foreign training in different countries; some includes, Malaysia, China, Indonesia, Sri Lanka, Philippines, Singapore, Turkey, Japan, Vietnam, India, UK, Canada, and Thailand. In addition he has conducted several research projects for the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare of Bangladesh, NGOs and private agencies. Earlier he worked with different international development organization. He attended several capacity building training workshop and seminar around the world. He has completed his MBBS and MPH from Bangladesh and MA in Population and Reproductive Health Research from the IPSR, Mahidol University of Thailand.
